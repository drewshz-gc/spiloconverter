== Entscheidung

Zwei Repositories, klare Zustaendigkeiten:

* *`spiloconverter`* — Java-Tool `spilo2cnpg` (Code, Tests, CLI-Referenz)
* *`cloud-native-postgres-operator`* — CNPG-Migrationsprojekt (Projektplan, Runbooks, Konzepte, Publish)

== Was liegt wo

[cols="2,3,1"]
|===
| Thema | Fuehrendes Repo / Pfad | Andere Repos

| Tool `spilo2cnpg` (Code) | `spiloconverter` *hier* | —
| CLI-Referenz `spilo2cnpg` | `docs/spilo2cnpg.adoc` *hier* | Spiegel in `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/tooling/`
| Projektplan, Jira-Abgleich, Migration | `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/migration/` | `spiloconverter/docs/` — Redirect-Stubs
| Betriebs-Runbook Pilot tifa | `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/betrieb/` | `selfservice-test-apps/.../tifa/` — Manifeste im GitOps-Repo
| Backup-Konzept, Vergleiche, Labels | `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/konzepte/` | `postgres-documentation/` — Archiv
| Condor-Referenz-Manifeste | `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/examples/condor/` | `spiloconverter/examples/condor/` — Redirect-Stubs
| Confluence CNPG-Hub | `cloud-native-postgres-operator` (`scripts/publish_confluence.py`) | —
|===

== Regeln

. *Tool-Aenderungen* (Java, Tests, `docs/spilo2cnpg.adoc`) in `spiloconverter`.
. *Projekt-Aenderungen* (Plan, Runbook, Konzepte) in `cloud-native-postgres-operator/documentation/`.
. *Generator-Output:* ein YAML-Dokument pro Datei (`kind: Cluster` only), kein Multi-Document (`---`), keine `Database`-CRDs.
. Companion-Ressourcen (`ObjectStore`, `Pooler`, `PodMonitor`, `ScheduledBackup`) tenant-spezifisch ausserhalb des Generators pflegen.
. Deploy-Manifeste bleiben im jeweiligen GitOps-Repo.

== Schnellstart (Tool)

[source,bash]
----
cd spiloconverter
cat README.adoc
cat docs/spilo2cnpg.adoc
mvn -q package -DskipTests
java -jar target/spiloconverter-*.jar --help
----
